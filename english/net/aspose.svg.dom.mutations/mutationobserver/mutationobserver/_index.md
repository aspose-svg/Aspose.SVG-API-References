## MutationObserver constructor

Constructs a MutationObserver object and sets its [`MutationCallback`](../../mutationcallback/) to callback. The callback is invoked with a list of MutationRecord objects as first argument and the constructed MutationObserver object as second argument. It is invoked after nodes registered with the [`Observe`](../observe/) method, are mutated.
